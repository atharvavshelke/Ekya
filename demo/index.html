How It Works

The complete encryption pipeline from keystroke to relay.

1

CRDT Mutation

Your edit produces an Operation with a deterministic opId (SHA-256) and causal dependencies (vector clock).

→
2

Serialize

The operation is packed into compact binary using MessagePack.

→
3

Encrypt

AES-256-GCM with a random 12-byte IV wraps the binary into an opaque ciphertext blob.

→
4

Relay

The trustless server forwards the encrypted envelope. It cannot read it.

→
5

Decrypt & Merge

The peer decrypts, deserializes, and applies the operation to their local CRDT. Convergence is guaranteed.
